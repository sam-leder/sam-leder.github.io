Scoring system


The schemes are scored as follows. For every question number between 2 and 8, a score between 0 and 5 is given to each scheme. This score depends on your answer, with higher scores given to schemes that fit best with your answer. If you decide to answer questions marked as expert questions, these take precedence over the regular question covering the same topic. Questions marked important count double. At the end, each final score is normalized to be between 0 and 100 and displayed, as it is above.
